Fashion · Brand Identity from Scratch
Crocodile
Crocodile had content. It just didn't have a point of view. When I came in, menswear on Instagram was a sea of sameness — everyone doing the same clean white backgrounds, the same generic lifestyle shots. So we went the other way. We made Crocodile the hero — not the clothes. The name, the legacy, the attitude. Editorial shifts, stronger typography, a visual language that felt like it had something to say. And then we took it further — moved away from shoots entirely and directed designers to build an AI content pipeline that honestly looks better than what we were producing on set. Less budget.
